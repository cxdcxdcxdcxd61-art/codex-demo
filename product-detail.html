Origin Story

Sourced in shadow. Roasted for gold.

Midnight Blend begins with producers who privilege slow maturation, careful sorting, and expressive sweetness. NOIRÉ composes the blend from Brazil Cerrado natural lots for black-cacao density, Ethiopia Sidama selections for dark-berry lift, and Colombia Huila washed coffees for architectural clarity.

Each component is roasted in small nocturnal batches, then rested and cupped against a single philosophy: restraint before spectacle, depth before sweetness, and a golden finish that feels tailored rather than loud.

Brewing Experience

Grind immediately before serving, warm black porcelain, and pour into a low-lit cup. Espresso reveals a lacquered crema; French press opens into cacao velvet; pour-over finishes with dark berry and a satin-gold sweetness.
